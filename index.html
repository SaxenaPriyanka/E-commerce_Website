Unveiling the Makers: The Art of Hand-crafted Jewellery

Lady selling Hand-crafted jewellery

Sushila (left) turned to crafting bead and stone jewelry by the roadside after the loss of her husband, becoming the sole provider for her three children. Despite the hardships, each piece she fashions is not just an exquisite accessory but a symbol of her unwavering determination. Through the challenges of roadside vending and the weight of her responsibilities, Sushila's unbreakable spirit is a testament to the power of perseverance.

Asha (right) creates handcrafted bead and stone jewelry right by the roadside, eking out a living for her family through her craftsmanship. She meticulously crafts each piece with her skilled hands, pouring her heart into every delicate detail. In the midst of the bustling roadside market, Asha's unwavering dedication shines brightly, a testament to her indomitable spirit.

Beyond the Kiln: Artisanal & Exquisite Craftsmanship in Ceramics

Hand-embroidery work on Chikankari piece

Meet the two Chinese brothers, Li Wei and Li Jian, masters of the ancient ceramic craft, whose hands mold clay into exquisite dinnerware. With decades of shared experience, they labor meticulously, shaping each piece from raw materials, a testament to their unwavering dedication to the art. Their brotherly bond, rooted in tradition, resonates in every ceramic masterpiece they craft. Through their work, they not only preserve a centuries-old heritage but also contribute to the cultural tapestry of our world, making every ceramic piece a symbol of history and craftsmanship.
